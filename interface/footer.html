DEAL'TJE
Founded in Eindhoven in 2018. Dealtje was created as an answer to the luxury trend of over-abundant branding and excessive pricing. Our designs do not confine to fashion - we simply aspire to provide high quality, well-designed essentials for the right price. We work with a minimalistic philosophy, as we believe sustainability and durability are better for us and our world.
